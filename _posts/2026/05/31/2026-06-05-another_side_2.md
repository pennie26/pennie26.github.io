## Chapter 1: Angel - con.
Except for sports and physical activities. She’s terrible at those. But we need to look at the bright side! Okay, maybe I’m oversharing. I’ll let Devil do the rest of the work in his chapter. 

Back to the story. I've been telling you about the person I got assigned to, but you haven’t known about me yet. My name is Angel. I was born under the name of God, to keep this world safe and sound, to keep the balance between the good and the bad. 

So, what is  good and what is bad? I don’t think I have an answer for this. Is killing considered bad? I don’t know. Is loving considered good? I don’t know either. This world is full of mysteries. And we can’t solve all of them, so right now, we’ll be focusing on solving the problem about our trouble-maker - our l’il Yugi here. 

She’s sitting, listening to music and working on creative writings that she is SO good at. Her Japanese notebook is open, with all sorts of vocabularies and sentence structures filled all over the page. What a hard worker. All thanks to me. I hope we can continue keeping her like this until Devil takes his place and everything will be a mess again.

## Inquiry
  This inquiry is a summative assessment for my Social Studies class, and we have to do researchs and write 2 PEEL paragraphs about the Dawn Raids or the Chinese Immigration in the 19th Century to New Zealand. Most of my friends chose the Dawn Raids because we've learned a little bit about it before, but I chose the Chinese one, as I like to be different. <3

## Focus Questions: 
# - HOW and WHY did your group immigrate to Aotearoa?
# - What were the impacts of cultural interaction on your group and society?
